**Purpose:** prove creation works

### 2️⃣ GET by ID – success case ✅ (you already have this)

**Purpose:** prove retrieval works

**Test logic:**

*   POST event
*   GET `/api/events/id/{id}`
*   Expect `200 OK`

✅ You effectively have this already.

### 3️⃣ POST – validation failure ❗ (important)

**Purpose:** prove your validation + global error handling works

**Test logic:**

*   Expect `400 Bad Request`
*   Optionally assert `fieldErrors` exists

This is a **Capstone must-have** test.
